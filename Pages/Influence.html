Influences

Never Gives Up

Gon is stubborn and doesn't back down, even if it means losing his life. He struggles with being perceived as weak and wants to prove his worth.


Gon cares about fairness and won't accept losing if the loser has to forfeit.


Gon gives up his future potential to defeat Neferpitou. He makes a Nen contract that allows him to sacrifice all his Nen to kill Neferpitou. The result is his body maturing into that of a muscular adult with immense power and Nen aura.


Gon's choice reflects his deep emotional investment in the story and the consequences of his actions.

Caring

Gon is always ready to protect his friends and avenge them. For example, he protects a Foxbear cub with his own life and doesn't let Kite harm it.


Gon is kind, compassionate, and forgiving of everyone, even if they've wronged him. For example, he chooses to heal a psychopath who had just attempted to kill him.


Gon is empathetic, taking on the burdens and duties of those around him until their goals become equally as important as his own.


Gon tries to be as kind as possible until he finally makes his decision on whether someone will be beneficial to him in the future. For example, he doesn't care about someone trying to kill him if the person interests him.

Hard Working

Gon has talent beyond the norm for his age. He is an extremely fast learner and is able to hold his own as a kid who only knew the basics for a long period of time.


Gon listens to others and follows through on training. He practices often.


Gon has almost inhuman willpower. He is stubborn about this and never backs down even if it costs him his life. He wants to prove his worth and feel that he's not weak.


Gon continually strives to obtain greater power, often becoming exceptionally stronger in his anger. His anger also leads to some extremely reckless tendencies.

Physically Fit

Gon's training may also contribute to his physical strength. He has been trained by multiple Hunters in both Nen and combat, and grew up in the mountains, where he unknowingly trained through his activities.


Gon's senses are so sharp due to his training and time spent in the forests of Whale Island. He also has enhanced strength, durability, and endurance.


He has superhuman abilities!
